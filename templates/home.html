What is Yggdrasil?

In Norse mythology, Yggdrasil is the great Tree of Life — its roots reach into the past, its branches stretch toward the future.

Inspired by this timeless symbol, Yggdrasil is a digital space where you can explore your own family story — one connection, one memory, one relationship at a time.

Whether you're tracing your lineage, preserving stories for future generations, or simply mapping out the people who shaped your life — Yggdrasil gives you a clean, private, and intuitive way to visualize your family tree.
